About Us

Welcome to our family-owned bulldog breeding business! We are based in Santa Ana, serving our community with love since 2018. We specialize in raising high-quality bulldogs, ensuring each puppy's best care from birth to their new homes. Our family is passionate about bulldogs and committed to closely working with our customers to find their perfect new family. With our great knowledge of puppy care and offering stud services, we provide an all-around exceptional experience. Choose us for a seamless transition into a lifetime of happiness with your new bulldog companion.

FAQ

When can I take My puppy home?

You will be able to get your puppy home once they are accustomed to dry food and water which happens at around 8 weeks old

Are the Puppies Vaccinated and Dewormed?

We adhere to veterinary guidelines, ensuring that your puppy receives three sets of essential vaccinations and two courses of deworming treatments prior to adoption.

Are the Puppies Registered?

All of our bulldogs are pure bread and registered by the American kennel Club (AKC)

Can I meet the puppies parents?

Certainly you are welcome to come meet the parents just give us a call!

Are the puppies potty trained?

Certainly, we initiate potty training with the puppies, but it's important to retrain them to adapt to your specific schedule and surroundings.

Can I reserve my puppy?

Yes you can, keep in mind that there is a holding fee.

What is your guaranteeon the puppies health?

We provide a one-year health guarantee for the puppies, as it's highly unusual for them to develop a serious condition after the first year.

How long have you been breeding?

We have been breeding bulldogs hapily as a family for 1 year.
